Got Questions?

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS.

Is there a sign-up or joining fee?+
No — there is no sign-up fee or joining fee for any of our memberships. You only pay the weekly membership rate.
Can I cancel my membership?+
Open memberships (no contract) can be cancelled with 2 weeks notice. Contract memberships have a minimum term — please check your agreement for details.
What's included in the membership?+
All memberships include full 24/7 access to all gym facilities, group classes, and the Kids Zone. Personal training is available as an add-on with our trainers.
Can I put my membership on hold?+
Yes, membership holds are available for medical reasons or extended travel. Please contact us directly to arrange.
How does 24/7 access work?+
Once you're a member, you'll receive a key fob or access card that lets you enter the facility at any time of day or night. The gym is monitored by 24/7 surveillance cameras.
